Dr Seshagiri Rao
Director of the Satish Dhawan Space Centre, ISRO

Dr. Seshagiri Rao Vellanki, an esteemed Indian scientist, boasts an illustrious career spanning over 39 years within the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO). His contributions have been instrumental in various capacities, notably as the former Distinguished Scientist and Associate Director of the Satish Dhawan Space Centre (SDSC). During his tenure, he played a pivotal role in establishing cutting-edge Real Time Systems and sophisticated computer networks, enhancing flight safety protocols at SDSC SHAR.

Moreover, Dr. Vellanki's expertise extends to the development of crucial data processing software essential for visualizing flight trajectories, ensuring precise monitoring and analysis. Under his leadership, significant advancements were made in the domain of Pulse Coherent Mono-pulse Radars (PCMC), with successful development and implementation. His visionary approach and technical acumen facilitated the integration of state-of-the-art technologies, bolstering ISRO's capabilities in mission-critical areas.

Furthermore, Dr. Vellanki's contributions transcend conventional boundaries, as evidenced by his involvement in the development and implementation of algorithms for wind profile estimation, crucial for weather monitoring systems. His dedication to enhancing data analytics and simulation methodologies has significantly enriched ISRO's capabilities, enabling more accurate mission simulations and post-flight trajectory estimations.

Overall, Dr. Seshagiri Rao Vellanki's multifaceted expertise and leadership have left an indelible mark on ISRO's trajectory, shaping its technological landscape and ensuring its continued success in space exploration endeavors.
