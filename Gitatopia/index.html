Frequently Asked Questions

Get all your queries answered here

  • How can I participate in Gitatopia 2024?

    To participate, you can register through our official website. Follow the registration process and ensure you meet the eligibility criteria.

  • Is Gitatopia open to students from all colleges?

    Yes, Gitatopia welcomes students from all colleges and universities. As long as you have a passion for technology and innovation, you're encouraged to participate.

  • What are the benefits of attending Gitatopia?

    Attending Gitatopia offers networking opportunities, skill development sessions, exposure to innovative ideas, and the chance to interact with industry professionals.

  • How can my organization become a partner for Gitatopia?

    If your organization is interested in partnering with Gitatopia, please contact us through the provided channels on our website. We're open to exploring collaboration opportunities.
